Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

I

Our Philosophy

At Paper Hallway Studio, we believe your data is your property. Our tools are designed to be “Local-First.” We do not want your data, we do not store your files, and we do not sell your information.

II

Aether (Desktop)

Aether operates entirely on your local machine. Your documents, PDFs, and project files never leave your computer. The AI intelligence is powered by local processing; no data is sent to our servers for analysis.

III

Inkwell (Mobile)

Inkwell processes voice notes to generate project intelligence. While we use secure APIs for high-fidelity synthesis, your raw audio is never stored permanently on our servers. Once the synthesis is complete, the document is yours to archive.

IV

Payments (Stripe)

We use Stripe for payment processing. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. Stripe’s privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information.

V

Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is nothing for us to delete. You are in total control of your “Office” and your “Inkwell.”
